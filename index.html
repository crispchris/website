Christoffer Löffler

I am a PhD student at MaD Lab and Fraunhofer IIS where I work on machine learning for time series data.

At Fraunhofer I've worked on optical positioning, complex event processing, and large scale virtual reality. I did my Bachelors and Masters at Friedrich-Alexander University Erlangen-Nürnberg (FAU), where I was advised by Christopher Mutschler.
